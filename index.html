FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Can foreigners visit? Is English OK?
Absolutely. We warmly welcome all foreign guests. All therapists speak English — no Japanese required at any point during your visit.
Can I book on the same day?
Yes, same-day bookings are available subject to availability. We recommend booking in advance to secure your preferred therapist and time slot.
Is my privacy completely protected?
Yes. All sessions take place in fully private rooms with no security cameras. Your visit is 100% confidential. We never share guest information.
What payment methods are accepted?
Cash only (JPY). We do not accept credit cards, debit cards, IC cards, QR codes, or any electronic payments. Please bring sufficient cash. The exact amount is confirmed at the time of booking.
How does the Google Review coupon work?
Create a free Google account, search for "Massage Tokyo" on Google Maps, and post an honest review. Show us your posted review at time of payment and receive ¥2,000 off your session. One coupon per person, cannot be combined with other offers. Cash payment required.
Where are you located?
We have two locations: Ginza and Kayabacho. The exact address for your chosen location is provided upon booking confirmation.
What are your prices?
Our sessions are priced as follows: 80 min ¥42,000 / 100 min ¥52,000 / 130 min ¥62,000. Time extensions are available at ¥8,000 per 10 minutes. All payments are cash only (JPY).
Do you provide adult or sexual services?
No. We are a relaxation salon specializing exclusively in aroma oil massage. We do not provide any form of adult or sexual services. Any violation of our terms will result in immediate termination of the session with no refund.
